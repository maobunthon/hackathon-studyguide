The UP's Profile

General information

University of Puthisastra is to develop, share and provide knowledge and competency through our core values to the students, so that they can take part in the ownership of the development process of Cambodia and the global community.

Mission

The mission of UP to develop and transfer knowledge, increase capacity to learn, and provide a chance to realize ones' full potential. Through experience based education, research, and sharing with national and international partners, we aim to produce qualified professionals, and entrepreneurs imbued with the love of learning.

Story

We offer a wide range of study program include the following Faculties:

  1. Arts and Humanities Faculty (Business, Economics, English)
  2. Social Sciences and Law Faculty (Law)
  3. Rural Development and Agriculture (Rural Development and Agriculture)
  4. Sciences and Technology (Networking, Database and Programming)
  5. Faculty of Health Science (Bachelor Degree in Medical Doctor, Phamarcy, Dentistry, Nursing, and Associate Degree in Midwife, Lab, and Nursing)
