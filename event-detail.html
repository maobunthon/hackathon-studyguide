Hackathon SVtoPP

Post by: Puthisastra University

Overview

8 teams of 3-5 members are invited to compete in the first annual Hackathon #SVtoPP and a shot at winning attractive prizes including mentorship from successful tech entrepreneurs based in both Cambodia and Silicon Valley. Selected teams will be challenged to create new products or services to solve real-world problems, or just something disruptively awesome.

Date and Location

- Start: 7:00 Saturday, Nov. 26th
- End: 15:00 Sunday, Nov. 27th
- Location: The University of Puthisastra, Phnom Penh

Prizes and Recognition

- Best Project – $500USD, press mention, and mentorship
- Outstanding Team – $200USD, press mention, and mentorship
- Promising Team – press mention and mentorship
All participating teams are invited to continue to work with members of Cambodians in Tech who can provide experience and advice to start a successful business.
